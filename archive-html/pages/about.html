Meet Jay Hedley

Co-founder of The Coaching Room and creator of the Integral+ High-Performance Model. 20+ years of elite mental performance coaching at the highest levels of sport.

Philosophy

Jay's developmental approach helps athletes and coaches "wake up, grow up, clean up, and show up" to their full potential. This holistic methodology addresses the whole person – mind, body, and leadership – not just performance symptoms.

Rather than treating mental skills as an add-on to physical training, Jay integrates psychological development into the complete performance system. The result is athletes who are mentally prepared for anything competition throws at them.

Unique Approach

Unlike run-of-the-mill sports psychology, Jay's Integral model systematically trains mental strength, conditioning, technique, tactics, and strategy. This comprehensive framework has been championship-proven at the highest levels of sport—from Olympic Games to Formula 1, World Cups to professional leagues.

The Journey

Foundations

Jay began his coaching journey over two decades ago, driven by a fascination with what separates good performers from great ones. Early work in NLP and coaching laid the groundwork for what would become the Integral+ model.

The Coaching Room

Co-founding The Coaching Room allowed Jay to develop and refine his methodology through work with executives, leaders, and high performers across industries—insights that would prove valuable when applied to elite sport.

Elite Sport

Bringing the Integral+ approach to professional sport, Jay began working with elite athletes and teams. Early successes led to opportunities with national programs, professional teams, and individual world-class athletes.

Championship Results

Olympic gold, World Cup performances, F1 podiums—the results validated the approach. Today, Jay works with champions and aspiring champions across multiple sports, continuing to refine and apply the Integral+ model at the highest levels.
