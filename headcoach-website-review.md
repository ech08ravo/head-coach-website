## Overview
Review of three pages on headcoach.coach for UX and content improvements.

**Pages Reviewed:**
- Homepage (headcoach.coach)
- Integral+ Advantage (/integral-plus-advantage/)
- Mental Skills Framework (/mental-skills-framework/)

---

## Key Findings

### Strengths
- Strong expertise positioning and credentials (Olympic medals, 20+ years experience)
- Clear service offerings and methodology differentiation
- Professional tone balancing academic rigor with accessibility
- Good use of case studies (Fiji Rugby 7s)

### Issues
- Important trust signals (credentials, client logos) buried too far down pages
- Heavy use of expandable +/- toggles hides valuable content
- Academic/jargon-heavy content on Integral+ page without early definitions
- Missing visuals referenced in content (concentric circles diagram)
- No persistent navigation menu across site
- Testimonials lack context (sport/role)
- Dense text walls on longer pages
